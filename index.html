The ZERO Rule
Pattern + Key Level + 5/5 Score + 15M Sweep = Signal. A pattern at a random location is noise. The same pattern at your planned entry zone after a liquidity sweep is edge.
Anatomy of a candle
Green = close above open (buyers won). Red = close below open (sellers won). Wicks show how far price was rejected before closing.
Three questions before acting on any pattern
01 Where is it forming? At a key level or in empty space?
02 Does the higher timeframe agree? Daily + weekly aligned?
03 What does the next candle do? Always wait for confirmation.
Reliability tiers
HIGH — Engulfing, Morning/Evening Star, Three White Soldiers, Three Black Crows. Multi-candle, self-confirming.
MEDIUM — Hammer, Shooting Star, Pin Bar, Piercing Line. Need next-candle confirmation.
CAUTION — Doji, Spinning Top, Harami. Indecision only — never enter on these alone.
